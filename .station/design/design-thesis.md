# Design Thesis

## Aesthetic Direction: Pit Lane Industrial

The visual language of F1 timing screens and pit wall monitors. Dark, dense, precise. Every element earns its place. Information over decoration; tension over calm.

The one thing someone will remember: it feels like a pit wall display, not a fantasy sports app.

## Four Core Rules

1. **Density with hierarchy** — pack information, but make the reading order unambiguous.
2. **State is sacred** — live, locked, and upcoming states must be visually distinct at a glance.
3. **One-handed by default** — all primary actions live in the bottom 40% of the screen.
4. **Race weekend rhythm** — the app has three moods: dormant (no active race), active (prediction window open), and live (race in progress). UI intensity should match.
